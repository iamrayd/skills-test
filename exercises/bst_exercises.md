# BST Exercises

1) Insert this sequence into a BST: **50, 30, 70, 20, 40, 60, 80, 30**.  
   a) Give inorder, preorder, postorder, and level-order.  
   b) What is the height? (Height of a single-node tree is 0.)

2) Search for **60** and **25** in your tree from (1). Count comparisons.

3) Insert **15** and **55** into the tree from (1). What is the new inorder?

4) Starting from the tree in (1), remove keys in this order: **70, 30, 50**.
   After each removal show the inorder and draw the tree.
